Instructions

  • You can type a pokemon name in the search box in the upper right to search for them and add them to the playfield.
  • You can click the deck in the upper right corner of the play field to draw a random pokemon.
  • You can have a maximum of 5 pokemon on your bench at a time.
  • If you have 5 on the bench and draw another, the one on the left end of your bench will move to the discard pile.
  • You can drag your strongest pokemon to the Active spot, centered above the bench
  • You can also drag any unwanted pokemon to the discard pile and you will automatically draw a new one.
  • All pokemon are generated with random abilities and stats. Hover over your pokemon to see what their stats are.
  • To access these insructions or reset the playfield, look in the Help menu.
